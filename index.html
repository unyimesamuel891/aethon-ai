What AETHON does

Built for serious creators.

Every tool is designed to get out of your way. No clutter, no mystery. Just the work.

01
Multi-modal generation
Images, long-form text, music tracks, 3D assets, and short video — all in one interface. Switch modalities without leaving the page.
02
Process visualization
Watch every diffusion step in real time. Intervene at any point to steer the output. Transparent by design — no black box.
03
Fine-grained parameter control
CFG scale, step count, seed locking, negative prompts, resolution, and style conditioning — every variable exposed and adjustable.
04
Prompt library and marketplace
Save, version, and sell your best prompts and style presets. Browse community-contributed templates. Prompts are diff-able.
05
Real-time collaboration
Invite collaborators into a shared session. See cursors, share prompt history, and iterate on the same output together with full version tracking.
06
Licensing and commercial export
Every output ships with a clear, tiered license. Export PNG, SVG, MP4, GLB, or MIDI. Commercial use included from the Creator plan upward.
